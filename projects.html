2K1 In Silico

2K1 In Silico is a project with Gary King at Harvard's Institute for Quantitative Social Science. The project serves as a tool to teach statistics without the need for students to know how to code.

Survey Reporting

As a research assistant for Chase Harrison, I worked on a project to analyze how articles report the use of surveys in top academic journals. The paper is currently under review.
